Despondent

The year we didn't celebrate Christmas

by Kabir Singh

Deficient is me this year,
Deficient is December this year,
Deficient is Christmas this year,
Deficient is Christmas without the opening of Charity box,
Deficient is Christmas without the decoration of Board,
Deficient is Christmas without the singing of carol,
Deficient is Christmas without the bustling school,
Deficient is Christmas with the Campus Picnic,
Deficient is Christmas without the rum cake,
Deficient is Christmas without that small Class dance,
Deficient is Christmas without the benevolent acts,
Deficient is every Marisian.

Despondent is my heart without that last Christmas December in School,
Despondent is my heart at my country's peace,
Irked I am at the deranged riots.

Hope I have now, for the King is born,
Hope I have now, for merry days adorn,
Hope I have now, because the Lord has blesse
